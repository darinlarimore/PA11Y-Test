Volunteers make Rev possible. Without your help, Rev could not offer an amazing experience to guests or make a lasting impact on Central Indiana.

Benefits to volunteering for Rev:

  • For a great cause!
  • Have fun and meet others from the Indianapolis area
  • Exclusive behind-the-scenes volunteer orientation at IMS prior to the event
  • Receive a special thank you volunteer gift
  • Access to the Volunteer Hospitality Room during the event
  • Receive a free Rev T-shirt to wear while you volunteer
  • If you volunteer for a four-hour shift either Friday or before 6:00 pm Saturday, you will have the opportunity to purchase discounted Premiere tickets at $150.00 each (limit two)

Volunteer opportunities

A flexible schedule lets you find the volunteer opportunity that fits with your time and skills.
Volunteer to help set up before the event, keep things running during Rev or clean up afterward.
